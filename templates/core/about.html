About South East Archers

Our Mission

South East Archers is dedicated to promoting the sport of archery and providing a welcoming community for archers of all skill levels.

What We Offer

  • Regular Shooting Nights: Join us for shooting events at our various locations including the Hall, Meadow, and Woods.
  • Member Memberships: Get a membership with included credits for shooting events.
  • Credit System: Use credits to participate in shooting nights, or purchase additional credits as needed.
  • Community Events: Participate in special events and competitions throughout the year.

Getting Started

If you're new to archery or want to join our community, here's how to get started:

  1. Register an account with your email address.
  2. Receive a membership with 20 initial credits.
  3. Check out our upcoming events and latest news.
  4. Join us for a shooting night!
