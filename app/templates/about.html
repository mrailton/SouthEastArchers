About South East Archers

Our Club

South East Archers is a premier archery club dedicated to promoting the sport of archery in the South East region. Founded with a passion for precision and excellence, we provide a welcoming environment for archers of all skill levels.

Whether you're a beginner looking to try archery for the first time or an experienced archer seeking to refine your skills, our club offers the facilities, coaching, and community to help you achieve your goals.

Facilities

  • Modern indoor and outdoor archery ranges
  • Equipment rental available for beginners
  • Professional coaching and training programs
  • Safe, well-maintained facilities
  • Clubhouse with amenities

Membership

Our annual membership is just €100 and includes 20 shooting credits. Each credit grants you access to one evening of shooting at our facilities. Additional credits can be purchased at any time for €5 per credit through your member dashboard.

Members enjoy access to all club events, competitions, training sessions, and our supportive community of fellow archers.
